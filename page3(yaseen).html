The bee population has been on the decline for many years due to a multitude of different factors. Poor farming practices, changes in weather patterns, loss of habitat, pesticide use, and diseases are some of the reasons for this decline. Moreover, bees are very important economically. Many products (honey, beeswax, pollen) are used to make a variety of goods. To preserve the good bees do, we must act now.
